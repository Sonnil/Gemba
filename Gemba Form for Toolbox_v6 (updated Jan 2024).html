Photo and/or video guidance for Single Use Technology:

Criticality Assessment

Impacted or Potential Impact on Product?

 

Select YES:

•  When we cannot rule out that the deviation will have an impact on the safety, quality, identity, potency or purity of a product or the

performance of a device.

 

• When we work outside the validated range for critical parameters. The

process control strategy can be used as a guideline for this.

Extra Notes:

Safety, Patients, Data Reliability?

 

Select YES in the event of non-compliance with the content of the documents Sanofi has submitted to regulatory authorities or if we fail a

requalification.

Extra Notes:

Is The Event Indicative Of A Trend?

 

Select YES, when a trend is detected during anomaly trending, quarterly deviation trending, laboratory tests, complaints, etc.

Extra Notes:

Impact GxP And/Or Regulatory Compliance?

 

See below for some examples of when YES should be selected:

• Failure of controls within our system or lack of controls

• When we don't follow the change-control process.

• When we use processes, equipment or systems that are not qualified/validated.

• GxP activities performed by person not qualified for tasks

• Data Integrity Issues

• Data loss

• Pervasive systemic failure

• Gap in critical computerized system validation/cleaning, process validation

 

Note:  This is not an exhaustive list, just a high level guide
